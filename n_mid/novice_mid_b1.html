Mini Lesson: Counting by 10s and 100s - Reading

Counting by 10s: names of the numbers from 10 to 90 are as follows:

10: On - Ten

20: Yirmi - Twenty

30: Otuz - Thirty

40: Kırk - Fourty

50: Elli - Fifty

60: Altmiş - Sixty

70: Yetmiş - Seventy

80: Seksen - Eightly

90: Doksan - Ninety

Counting by 100s: name of the numbers from 100 to 900 are as below. Note that (a) hundred is "Yüz" but then similar to English, just like we say "two hundred" we put together the next number and "yüz", e.g. for two hundred "iki + yüz" = "iki yüz", and so on:

100: Yüz - (One) hundred

200: İki yüz - Two hundred

300: Üç yüz - Three hundred

400: Dört yüz - Four hundred

500: Beş yüz - Five hundred

600: Altı yüz - Six hundred

700: Yedi yüz - Seven hundred

800: Sekiz yüz - Eigh hundred

900: Dokuz yüz - Nine hundred

To say 1000: we say "Bin"

Mini Lesson: Counting by 10s and 100s - Writing

Now since we know how to count from 0 to 20 and from 10 to 900 we can start writing some mixed and different numbers. Try writing these numbers:

28, 34, 55, 89, 102, 113, 123, 290, 451, 704

Hint: The rules of making numbers 11 to 19 are valid for all other numbers. Examples:
21 = "yirmi + bir" -> yirmi bir
101 = "yüz + bir" -> yüz bir (we don't say the zero)
111 = "yüz + on + bir" -> yüz on bir
