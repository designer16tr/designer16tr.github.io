Mini Lesson: Future Tense - Reading

Future Tense is used for an action expected to happen in the future. An example in English is "I will go to the mall" Like other tenses we learned before, the first thing we need is the verb root and we obtain it by removing the "mek/mak" suffix from the infinite form. To create the Future Tense form we add one of the "ecek/acak" suffixes. If the last syllable of the verb root ends in a vowel we first add "y". Also in the 1st Singular and Plural pronouns, e.g. Ben and Biz the "k" in "ecek/acak" is substituted with "ğ"

Last Vowel of verb root  a/ı/o/u e/i/ö/ü
Ben-[y]acağım-[y]eceğim
Sen-[y]acaksın-[y]eceksin
O-[y]acak-[y]ecek
Biz-[y]acağız-[y]eceğiz
Siz-[y]acaksınız-[y]eceksiniz
Onlar-[y]acaklar-[y]ecekler
Suffixes to be added to the verb root

info item - explanation Ben

Here are some examples:
to write: yazmak - Ben yazacağım, Sen yazacaksın, O yazacak, Biz yazacağız, Siz yazacaksınız, Onlar yazacaklar
I will write, You will write, He/she/it will write, We will write, You will write, They will write

to see: görmek - Ben göreceğim, Sen göreceksin, O görecek, Biz göreceğiz, Siz göreceksiniz, Onlar görecekler
I will see, You will see, He/she/it will see, We will see, You will see, They will see

gelmek (to come)  Ben geleceğim, sen geleceksin, o gelecek, biz geleceğiz, siz geleceksiniz, onlar gelecekler
I will come, You will come, He/she/it will come, We will come, You will come, They will come

gülmek (to laugh)  Ben güleceğim, sen güleceksin, o gülecek, biz güleceğiiz, siz güleceksiniz, onlar gülecekler
I will laugh, You will laugh, He/she/it will laugh, We will laugh, You will laugh, They will laugh


Turkish grammar has few exceptions and we will see one of them below. Because the root of "yemek" is "ye" we have to add a "y", but practically there is no turkish word which contains the syllable "yey" so the "e" is substituted with "i":

yemek (to eat)  Ben yiyeceğim, Sen yiyeceksin, o yiyecek, biz yiyeceğiz, siz yiyeceksiniz, onlar yiyecekler
I will eat, You will eat, He/she/it will eat, We will eat, You will eat, They will eat
